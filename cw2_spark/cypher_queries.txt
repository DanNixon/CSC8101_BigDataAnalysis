For a given director, returns all Users who have rated more than one of their
movies at 4.0 or above:

MATCH
(:Director {name:"Lana Wachowski"})-[:DIRECTED]->(m:Movie),
(u:User)-[r:RATED]->(m)
WHERE r.stars >= 4.0
RETURN u

2)

For a given actor, return other actors with whom they have collaborated on more
than 3 movies:

MATCH
(:Actor {name:"Ben Stiller"})-[:ACTS_IN]->(m:Movie),
(a:Actor)-[:ACTS_IN]->(m)
WITH a, COUNT(m) as numMovies
WHERE numMovies > 3
RETURN a
